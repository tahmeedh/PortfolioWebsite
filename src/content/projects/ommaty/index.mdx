The Ommaty application provides a platform that allows restuarants and other businesses to advertise their products based on location. Helps muslims connect with other muslims for many different purposes. The app contains 4 main sections including a message system between users. An offers tab that will allow business to offer users special discounts exclusively from the app. The app will also include a newsfeed section, that will be paginating dynamically based to users posts. This application will bring many communities together and help businesses have a platform for their businesses.
