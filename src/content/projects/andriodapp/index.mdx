This applications implements a line of communication between personal trainers and their clients.The client and trainer are both able to input specific data based on if primary user or secondary user. The personal trainer is able to input the workout that are specific to the client in their individual profiles.The client is able to track their progress within the application putting emphasis on tracking your progression in your fitness journey. Giving visible feedback to create efficiency in the applications usability and creates consistency in following the specified routines given. A notification system displays a message that Android displays outside your app's UI to provide the user with reminders, communication from other people, or other timely information from your app.
